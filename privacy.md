# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Fetch collects account information, profile data, photos, location data, chat messages, and push notification tokens to provide app functionality.

We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider.

Location data is used to show nearby parks and location-based features while using the app.

Users may upload profile photos and communicate through chat features.

We may review reported content to enforce our community guidelines and keep the platform safe.

support@fetchconnect.app
